OUR HISTORY: OUR FAITH & GROWTH

Destiny Centre was founded in July, 2000 by Prophet Peter who was called by the Lord to raise men and establish them live in God's Kingdom. The church has always been known for it's great diligence in prayers, worship and breaking the yoke of enemies in the lives of others. Significant milestones in our ministry are local outreach programs, helping the poor, changing lives and many more. Under the leadership of the Founder(Prophet Peter Chiedu Odogwu) - we continue to pursue our mission.Today, our church is a vibrant and diverse community, welcoming individuals and families from all walks of life. We offer ministries for children, teens, adults, and seniors, with a focus on biblical teaching, heartfelt worship, and meaningful fellowship. Our church is very passinate in missionary work of God, showing him to the world bit locally and globally. Looking ahead, we are excited about the future. Our vision includes expanding our discipleship programs, investing in new facilities, and deepening our engagement with the community. As we celebrate our journey so far, we remain committed to walking in faith and being a light in Kubwa, believing that the best is yet to come.

MISSION & VISION

John 5:19 "Very truly I tell you, the Son can do nothing by himself; he can do only what he sees his Father doing, because whatever the Father does the Son also does." We replicate the ministry and life of Christ here on Earth. Our Church is called to unveil the kingdom of God to men. Not just in words but by practical demonstration of the supernatural(power and influence of the holy spirit.) We are also called to release destinies, war against forces fighting you and lead you towards realizing the purpose God hath created you for. The shackles that held the sick break, legal captives freed by the Blood of Jesus and Power given to every believer to transact the kingdom in their day to day lives.

OUR BELIEFS

At Destiny Centre(The Unveiling Chapel), we are a bible-believing curch committed to teachings of Christ and the truth of God's word. We believe in one God—Father, Son, and Holy Spirit—who is the Creator and Sustainer of all things. We believe that the Bible is the inspired and authoritative Word of God, guiding us in faith and daily living. As a community, we gather to worship, pray, learn, and encourage one another. We strive to be a light in our community, living out our faith with integrity, compassion, and purpose. Stay tuned and connected to know more.
